Who could be a final boss in a a [[Vagabonds]] legend?
Since this game is so defined by factions and player choice, each faction should have a *potential* BBEG. Also have a factionless BBEG. If there is somehow a connection between each of these BBEGs, it's easier to link them together and foreshadowing with hints becomes more effective.


### Formula
> Working backwards from the BBEG

1. BBEG
	- Flesh out what or who this is. Competent, wicked, must be stopped.
	- That means the BBEG should be evil. It's okay if lieutenants are morally gray or even anti-heroes, but the BBEG is evil.
2. High Tower Surprise
	- Build up to the confrontation.
	- Loyal Lieutenants
		- Mini-bosses, one for each portion of the story. These could be the potential BBEGs of a given faction, but if so, the subsequent BBEGs should grow accordingly in power and presence
		- Each lieutenant battle should leave breadcrumbs to another boss. This can evolve into a sort of nested campaign that grows as long as it's enjoyable. 
3. Big Goal
	- Bonus points if this is a cool twist
	- The bad guys want the same thing the players do
	- The bad guys want that which most disrupts [[The Woodland]]
4. Hints Along the Way
	- Build the myth
	- Plant subtle references to the BBEG
	- Sub-goals of the big goal that may not seem connected
	- Random encounters thematically attached to the BBEG
5. Inciting Incident
	- Start the adventure with a [[5 Room Dungeon]] that hints at a lieutenant
