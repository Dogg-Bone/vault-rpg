# The Sunken Tomb
In the forest between [[Barewick Clearing]] and [[Drytown Clearing]] lies an old decrepit crypt, it's entrance nestled in a grove of birch trees. 
[[Map of the Sunken Tomb]]
Steps lead down into a flooded chamber of rough-hewn stones. Seven pillars rise to the ceiling, the eighth fallen crosswise along the center of the room, rising above the surface of the water. The stagnant water carries a sickly sweet scent of rotting fruit. A portion of the wall to your right has caved in. Vines hang down over an irregular opening behind the fallen wall. Against the intact wall stands a stone statue of a solemn-faced mouse resting its hands on the pommel of a long, rusty metal sword stood on its point. An archway hangs over an exit at the far end of the room.
A [[Giant Centipede]] lurks in the water behind the fallen pillar.
