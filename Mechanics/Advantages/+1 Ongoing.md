**+1 Ongoing** is similar to +1 forward, but instead of applying only to the very next roll you make, it continues to be added to every roll that fits the move’s conditions. For example, a move saying “take +1 ongoing against the NPC” means you take +1 on each and every move you make that is “against” the NPC. “Ongoing” bonuses usually end either when the move says they do, or when the situation or scene has dramatically changed (GM’s call).
