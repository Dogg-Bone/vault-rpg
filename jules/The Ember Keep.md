# The Ember Keep

Deep within a dense, thorny bramble forest lies a ruined stone tower now repurposed by the Lord of Hundreds. Smoke billows constantly from the broken top, and the pungent smell of pitch and burning wood permeates the air for miles around.

### Room 1: The Charred Gates

The entrance to the keep is marked by two massive, scorched oak doors. They are barricaded from the inside. Atop the ruined walls flanking the gate stand two lookouts, scanning the treeline for interlopers. A massive bonfire burns just outside the gates, illuminating the approach. The ground is littered with the remnants of previous, less successful attackers. A [[Bandit]] mob acts as the guard here.

```statblock
creature: "Bandit"
```

### Room 2: The Zealot's Trial

Inside the gates is a courtyard turned into an impromptu arena. The walls are lined with cages holding prisoners from other factions. A charismatic mouse, known as the "Voice of the Fire", preaches to a small group of initiates. To pass without starting a massive brawl, the Vagabonds must either talk their way through by feigning allegiance to the Lord of Hundreds or solve the "Zealot's Trial" – a test of pain where they must hold a red-hot iron rod without flinching (requires rolling with Might, taking exhaustion on a partial hit or failure).

### Room 3: The Smoldering Gauntlet

A long hallway leading into the main keep is filled with thick, choking smoke. The floor is uneven, and several hidden pitfalls are filled with hot coals. Navigating this room requires rolling with Finesse or Cunning to avoid the traps. Taking too long means the Vagabonds will mark wear on their equipment or take exhaustion from the heat and smoke.

### Room 4: The Warlord's Throne

The heart of the keep is a massive, circular chamber that was once a noble's hall. The center of the room is dominated by a roaring fire pit. Sitting on a throne made of scavenged weapons and armor is a fearsome [[Lord of Hundreds Warlord]]. They are flanked by their elite bodyguards. The Warlord wields a terrifying, glowing blade.

```statblock
creature: "Lord of Hundreds Warlord"
```

### Room 5: The Ash Hoard

Behind the throne room is the Warlord's personal armory and treasure room. Unlike the rest of the keep, this room is relatively cool and quiet. Among the piles of scavenged supplies, stolen trinkets, and faction banners lies the prized possession of the Warlord, the [[Emberblade]].

```statblock
creature: "Emberblade"
```

There is also a hidden tunnel leading out of the keep, dug by previous occupants, providing a safe escape route for the Vagabonds once they have secured their loot.
